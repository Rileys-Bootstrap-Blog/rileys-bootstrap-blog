Provo Balloon Fest 2018

My time at the Provo City Balloon Fest

Posted by Riley Grigg on July 5, 2018 at 8:10 PM MDT

Arriving

We got to the balloon field at 6:00 AM on July 3rd. They hadn't started inflating the hot air balloons yet. It was my first time there so I didn't know what to expect but Deborah said usually they have them getting ready by then. They said they weren't going to be able to fly the balloons because of the wind going the wrong way but they did inflate them.
